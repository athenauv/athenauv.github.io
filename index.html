UV-C LEDs: The Future is Bright

With the synthesis of Indium-Gallium Nitride in 2014, it became possible for LEDs to emit germicidal UV-C light. A technology previously reserved for laboratories and hospitals is becoming safe, cheap, and portable for people everywhere. At Athena, we believe this technology will change the world, which is why we're building a new product to lead the way.
